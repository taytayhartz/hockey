Welcome to my assignment 6 Website

This website is about the St. Anselm Women's Ice Hockey team. It will provide a breif overview of the program, pictures and a highlight video from the teams game against Union College.

The Hawk's team plays at a highly competitive NCAA Division 1 level. It is the only Division 1 team on campus, being established in 2004. They compete in the NEWHA - New England Women's Hockey Alliance. This includes teams from other Universities and Colleges such as Post University, St. Michaels College, Sacred Heart University, Franklin Pierce University, Assumption University, Long Island University and Stonehill College. This season the Hawk's have taken on a lot of other tough competition outside of the league such as Boston College, University of Vermont, Yale, Union College, University of Maine, and Dartmouth. At the moment the Hawks are at the top of the NEWHA, ranking at number 1. The team just recently celebrated the programs 400th win! This is huge news for the program and history.

Saint Anselm's team is a fairly young team with 11 freshman. This season three transfer student joined the Hawks as sophmores, making the sophomore class expand to 7 players. The Junior class includes 6 players and the Senior class includes 5 players. The Hawks are lead by their three Senior captains. The team includes tallent from all over the World and United States. They have a couple of Canadians, as well as players from Michigan, California, Utah, Texas, New Mexico, Maine, Chicago and even New Hampshire.

The team puts a high emphasis on Teamwork and dedication both on the ice and in the classroom. Saint Anselm College enforces becoming a whole person and following the Bennedictine tradtions. These traits such as community and acceptance are implemented into the teachings of the College. Saint Anselm is an academically rigorous school as it prepairs these athletes for challenges that they will be facing with time and stress management once they join the career world.
